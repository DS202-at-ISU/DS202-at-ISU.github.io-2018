Our data set contains a list of video games with sales greater than 100,000 copies along with critic and user ratings. It is a combined web scrape from VGChartz and Metacritic along with manually entered year of release values for most games with a missing year of release. The original coding was created by Rush Kirubi

The exploration of this data sets out to answer several thought provoking questions.

  1. How do video game genres vary by console?
  2. How has the popularity of gaming genres changed throughout history?
  3. What are the top selling games each year?
  4. Do some games see more or less success based on region?
  5. What is the best selling video game of all time?
  6. What are the top games for each gaming platform?
  7. What is the lifespan of a gaming console and how many games were released throughout time?
  8. What is the market share distribution of consoles for each year?
  9. What is the relationship between critic score and platform?
  10. What impact do negative critic scores have on video game sales?

The project will delve into these questions, present the findings, and discuss methodology, when applicable.
